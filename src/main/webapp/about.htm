Who We Are

Guardian Angel Animal Rescue is a registered Michigan 501(c)(3) not-for-profit, no-kill rescue.   

 

We are a team of volunteers dedicated to saving animals from the streets, shelters, and dangerous situations. We take in animals that have been given up on, tortured, and often left to die.  Guardian Angel Animal Rescue (GAAR) has no formal shelter. Instead, we accept surrendered, abandoned, and/or abused animals into our homes and care for them until a responsible, loving, permanent home is found. Needless to say, there are some instances when all foster homes are full, and GAAR pays for animals to be professionally kenneled until there is an opening in a foster home.  We are always looking for more foster parents and other support volunteers to join our mission.
